Hello!
I am Michele Carson.

With a B.A. in Digital Technology and Culture from Washington State University's Vancouver Campus in the Creative Media and Digital Culture program, I specialize in User Experience (UX), User Interface (UI), and Web Development. In collaboration with the CMDC, I was the Lead UX/UI Designer for our project, Uncle Buddy's Phantom Funhouse.

In the CMDC, I have been exposed to all components of design and development.
This includes:

  • Content creation with the Adobe Creative Suite
  • Programming languages such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, SCSS, Bootstrap, and C#
  • Version Control Software and Cloud-Based Hosting with Git and GitHub
  • Game Development with Unity and HTML Canvas
  • User Experience and Interface Design with Adobe XD and Figma

I have also earned:

  • A Minor in Psychology
  • A Certificate in Game Studies and Design

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I look forward to working with you!
