Hello!
I am Michele Carson.

I am weeks away from graduating from Washington State University's Vancouver Campus in the Creative Media and Digital Culture program. In this program, I am specializing in User Experience (UX) & User Interface (UI) and I am the UX/UI lead for our current project, Uncle Buddy's Phantom Funhouse.

In my program, I have been exposed to all components of design and development. This includes content creation with the Adobe Creative Suite, programming languages such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, C#, and Bootstrap managed with GitHub, game creation with HTML Canvas and Unity, user experience and design paired with Adobe XD and Figma, and impact on culture.

To round out my skills, I am minoring in psychology to combine my practical design skills with a focus on human nature and habits while also earning a certificate in game studies and design.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I look forward to working with you!
